About Collatz Grapher

The Collatz Conjecture is simple: Take any number. If it's even divide it by two. If it's odd multiply it by three and add one. Wash, rinse, repeat. Eventually, the sequence lands on one, no matter the starting number. We hope you'll enjoy the unpredictability of the graphs that it generates!
